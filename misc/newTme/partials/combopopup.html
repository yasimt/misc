ECS Plan
For ECS plan: Against your selected monthly package Instant Bonus at the rate of 10% of Monthly ECS amount will be added to your JD Search package every month for 10 consecutive months.

Upfront Plan
For Upfront plan: Instant bonus equivalent to yearly Package amount will be credited to your yearly package.
Example:
Yearly package worth Rs. 24000/- will be equal to 48000/- (Yearly Package 24000/- + Bonus 24000/-)
